Brad Pitt

About

1963-present

William Bradley "Brad" Pitt was born on December 18, 1963 in Shawnee, Oklahoma and raised in Springfield, Missouri, the son of Jane Etta (Hillhouse), a school counselor, and William Alvin "Bill" Pitt, a truck company manager.

Major events

  1. Brad" Pitt was born on December 18, 1963 in Shawnee, Oklahoma
  2. Brad Pitt's acting career officially started in 1987 with uncredited roles in films and a television debut on Another World.
  3. In 1994, Pitt starred as the vampire Louis de Pointe du Lac in the horror film Interview with the Vampire: The Vampire Chronicles, which earned him the MTV Movie Award for Most Desirable Male and the MTV Movie Award for Best Actor in a Movie.
  4. Brad Pitt gained worldwide fame in the early to mid-1990s, transitioning from his breakout role in Thelma & Louise (1991) to solidify his status as a global superstar with roles in films like Legends of the Fall (1994) and Seven (1995).

Main achievements

  • Golden Globe Awards:for 12 Monkeys (1995) and Once Upon a Time... in Hollywood (2019)
  • Iconic film roles:in movies like Fight Club, Se7en, Interview with the Vampire, and A River Runs Through It
  • Successful films: produced by Plan B include The Departed, Moonlight, and The Big Short, all of which received Best Picture nominations or wins at the Academy Awards
